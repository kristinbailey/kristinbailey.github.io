Homestyle Cooking Straight from Jamaica

Jamaican Spice is available for all of your catering needs including graduation parties, wedding rehersals, work events, music festivals, etc. We can also incorporate our mobile food cart into your event serving hot dogs and hamburgers. Jamaican Spice will be operating roadside soon from Finesse Hair Salon in Ypsilanti, Michigan with plans to open in Ann Arbor, MI in the future.

About Us

Valancio Bailey, owner and chef, was born and raised in Jamaica. From age 12, after school each day, he would cook dinner for his family which translated a passion and love of cooking for all people. He arrived in Michigan in July 2015, and is excited to bring his own dishes to your table.

Bringing you dishes including, jerk chicken, rice and beans, brown stew chicken, vegetable rice, curry chicken, steam roast fish, fried plantains, curry goat, brown stew goat, brown stew pork, jerk pork, curry pork, stir fried chicken, jerk tofu, vegetable stew, banana fritters, vegetable fritters, stew fish, stewed peas with pigtail and beef, oxtail, coconut shrimp, steamed vegetables, fried chicken, BBQ chicken, BBQ wings, cheese potatoes, fruit salad, vegetable soup, coconut chicken noodle soup, and natural juices.
