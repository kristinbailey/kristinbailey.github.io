About Us

Valancio Bailey, owner and chef, was born and raised in Jamaica. From age 12, after school each day, he would cook dinner for his family which translated into a passion and love of cooking for all people. He arrived in Michigan in July 2015, and is excited to bring his own dishes to your table.
